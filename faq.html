Returns

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase from sailormoon.my, you may inform us within 7 working days of your intention to return the product and return the item(s) in its original condition together with its original receipt within 14 working days from the date you receive the product. We will provide a full refund in the original form of payment, provided the item(s) are unopened, unused and in their original packaging. We regret that we cannot make exchanges at this time.

Kindly follow the following steps to return your Sailor Moon Store.Co item(s) successfully:

  1. To initiate the return and refund process, you must first inform our Customer Service team at css@sailormoon.com.my within 7 working days from the date you receive the product with supporting evidence (picture of your item and its condition) and order number.
  2. Fill out the return form, to be provided by Customer Service via email, and place it inside the return package.
  3. Customer Service team will make an appointment with you to pick up the products. Our appointed shipping company will collect the product(s) from you at the arranged time.
  4. Re-pack the unused product(s) you wish to return in its original packaging, original receipt and include the return form inside the package. Kindly also include free gift/sample inside the package (if any).
  5. If you are entitled to a refund, we will process the calculated refund within thirty (30) days upon receipt of the returned product(s) in its original condition. Shipping fees or gift wrap is non-refundable.

Please note that you will have to cover the delivery cost (RM10) incurred for the return process, which will be charged to the same credit card used for making the purchase. You acknowledge and agree that we are authorised to offset such delivery costs from your purchase price and refund the balance to you.

 

Refunds will only be made against the original credit/debit card used. We regret that refunds for online banking is currently not supported. Only products purchased on sailormoon.my can be accepted for refund. Products purchased at sailormoon.my are not accepted for return at Sailor Moon Boutiques or department store locations.

 

If you have questions about returns, please contact us via email at css@sailormoon.com.my .

 

Damaged Items

When your order arrives, please inspect the carton for any damage that may have occurred during shipment. It is normal for the shipping carton to show some wear. However, if damage occurred to the item(s) in your shipment, please contact us immediately via email at css@sailormoon.com.my  with your order number and proof of evidence so that we can work with you to resolve the issue promptly.

To ensure a smooth resolution, please retain the shipping box, packing materials and the damaged items for inspection by the appointed shipping company.

Maximum Purchase Policy

We regret that we must limit orders to no more than 5 units for any item per order.
